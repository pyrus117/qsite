Education & Training

Building understanding and inclusion through workshops, training sessions, and resources for schools, organisations, and individuals across Nelson/Tasman.

School Support

We offer support for schools starting or running rainbow clubs or queer-straight alliances. We also assist with providing guidance or training for schools on rainbow support or issues.

Workplace Training

Professional development sessions for workplaces and organisations wanting to build a more inclusive and affirming environment. All at low cost to increase availability.

Community Training

Open community sessions on topics like pronoun use, allyship, supporting transgender youth, and understanding the rainbow community.

Enquire About Sessions →

Topics We Cover

  • LGBTQIA+ identities and terminology
  • Pronoun use and inclusive language
  • Supporting transgender and non-binary people
  • Creating safe and affirming environments
  • Takatāpui identity and te ao Māori perspectives
  • Bystander intervention and allyship

Queer Straight Alliances (QSAs)

Q Youth works in collaboration with Inside Out to support Queer Straight Alliances (QSAs) in secondary schools across the Nelson/Tasman region. QSAs are student-led groups that create inclusive spaces within schools.
