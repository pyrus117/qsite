Pekapeka Group

A monthly social space for queer young adults aged 18–30 in Nelson/Tasman, where you can build friendships, join community conversations, and be yourself.

Who We Are

The Pekapeka young adults group is a monthly gathering offering support, friendship, discovery, and empowerment for queer and questioning young adults in the Nelson/Tasman region.

We meet every month, usually on the third weekend, for social activities, community conversations, and to build lasting friendships in a relaxed and affirming environment.

3rd Sunday monthly

What We Offer

A space designed with young adults in mind — for connection, growth, and community.

Friendship & Connection

Build genuine friendships with other queer young adults in Nelson/Tasman. Find your people and create a supportive whānau.

Community Conversations

Monthly discussions on topics that matter to our community — from identity to mental health, relationships, and beyond.

Safe Space

A relaxed, non-judgmental environment to be yourself. Your identity and pronouns are respected and celebrated.

Want to Come Along?

Our next meeting is on the third Sunday of the month. Get in touch to find out more details or jump straight in!
