About Us

By Youth, For Youth

Q Youth is a charitable organisation (CC64299) based in Nelson, Aotearoa New Zealand. We exist to provide a safe, affirming environment where LGBTQIA+/Takatāpui young people feel valued, heard, and connected.

Young people are at the heart of everything we do; from the programmes we run to the decisions we make. Our team is deeply invested in the wellbeing and leadership of our community.

We serve the Nelson/Tasman region with drop-in groups, a young adults social group, education and training, local advocacy, and a wealth of resources for rangatahi and their whānau.
