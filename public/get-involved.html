Get Involved

There are many ways to support Q Youth — whether you want to volunteer, sit on our board, donate, or simply spread the word.

Ways to Get Involved

Volunteer with Us

Open Now

We're always looking for passionate people to join our volunteer team. Opportunities include: Tuakana-Teina peer mentoring, facilitation support at drop-ins, admin and communications, and more. We welcome Social Work and Counselling students looking for volunteer hours.

Apply to Volunteer

Board of Trustees

Enquire

Q Youth is governed by a dedicated Board of Trustees who meet monthly. We periodically open for new board members and welcome people with governance, legal, financial, community, or lived experience skills.

Apply for the Board

Support QSAs in Schools

Ongoing

Q Youth works with Inside Out to foster Queer Straight Alliances (QSAs) in secondary schools across our region. If you're interested in supporting this work at your school, get in touch.

Learn More

Help keep our doors open

Q Youth is a registered charity that relies on funding and donations to run our programmes. Every contribution directly supports rainbow rangatahi in our region.

Donate via GiveALittle

Make a one-off or recurring donation through our GiveALittle page. Every dollar goes directly to our programmes.

Buy something specific from our wishlist of items needed at the Q Youth centre. A practical way to make a tangible difference.

Spread the Word

Share our social media posts, tell friends and whānau about us, or put up a poster in your workplace. Awareness matters!

Rainbow Resource Packs
